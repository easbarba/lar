** Characters
   - characters is a question mark followed by the character
   #+begin_src elisp
   ?B
   ?A
   ?r
   ?R

   ;; escape sequences,
   ?\(
   ?\\

   ;; control-g, backspace, tab, newline, vertical tab, formfeed, space, return, del, and escape as
   ?\a   ?\b   ?\t   ?\n   ?\v   ?\f   ?\s   ?\r   ?\d ?\e
   #+end_src
